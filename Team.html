ABOUT US

Casabots is a competitive robotics team representing Morocco in international programs including FLL, FTC, and FRC. Our team brings together motivated students with strong skills in programming, engineering design, and mechanical systems to develop high-performance robotic solutions. Beyond competitions, Casabots is committed to community impact by sharing knowledge and promoting STEM education through outreach and training initiatives. With the support of our sponsors, we strive to empower youth, foster innovation, and build future technology leaders.
